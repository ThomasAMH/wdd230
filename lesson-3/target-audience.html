Targe Audience

Description of Audience

This page is designed for businesspeople in the Budapest area, and those looking to do business in the Budapest area, or with compaines in the Budapest areas. The page will cater to those who represent their companies, such as external relations managers, as well as other key business members.

Personas

  1. Primary User: Krisztina is a middle-aged business woman with a flower shop in the 12th district looking to network and connect with other business owners in district 12.
  2. Primary User: Lajos is an experienced male business owner looking to connect with other businesses in hopes of securing a new sourcing partner.

Usage Scenarios

  1. Somebody who does not know what a chamber of commerce page happens upon this: their local page.
  2. Business owners would like to connect with other businesses in Budapest that are in their industry.
  3. Those running for office would like to get information about the chamber's agenda.
  4. Business owners would like to join the chamber of commerce.
  5. A business owner would like to attend an upcoming event hosted by the chamber.
